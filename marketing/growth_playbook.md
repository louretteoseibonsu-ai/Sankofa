# Sankofa Twi — Growth & Install Playbook (@sankofatwi)

Goal: grow the IG audience **and** convert that attention into installed testers. Two different jobs — content grows reach, funnel converts it. Fix the funnel first, or you'll pour traffic into a leaky bucket.

---

## 0. Fix the install funnel FIRST (highest-impact)

Right now: IG → bio link → "Join the beta" email → you manually add them in Firebase → they wait for an invite → install. That's ~4 steps and hours of delay. It will kill your install rate at scale.

**Do this instead — make it one tap:**
- **Enable a Firebase App Distribution tester *invite link*** (Firebase Console → App Distribution → Testers & Groups → Invite links). Anyone with the link self-joins as a tester and installs — no manual add from you.
- OR move to **Google Play open testing** and use the public opt-in URL. (Note: Play now needs ~12 testers opted-in for 14 days before you can promote to production, so starting this clock early is smart.)
- Put that link behind a single **"Get the app (Android)"** button on sankofaapp.io, and that page as your **bio link**.
- Keep ONE call to action. No Linktree maze.

Everything below drives people to that one button.

---

## 1. Content engine — Reels are the only real reach lever

A new account grows on Reels, not feed posts. Aim **4–5 Reels/week** for the first 6–8 weeks.

Highest-potential Reel formats for you:
- **Sankofa Lens demos** — point camera → object named in Twi → say it. Your most shareable hook.
- **"Word of the day"** talking-head (you saying it, with the meaning + a Chale tip).
- **"Things you'll hear in Accra / at a Ghanaian house."**
- **Diaspora tries to speak Twi** / mispronunciation fixes (relatable, funny).
- **Reaction/duet** to Ghanaian pop culture, afrobeats, football moments.

Craft rules: hook in the first 1 second, burned-in captions (most watch muted), trending afrobeats/amapiano audio, 10–20s, one idea per Reel, end on the CTA.

Feed/carousels (your built kit) are for profile completeness + saves, not reach.

---

## 2. Discovery — get found by the right people

- **Name field SEO:** set your IG name to "Sankofa Twi · Learn Twi/Akan" — IG search indexes the *name* field, not just @handle.
- **Bio keywords:** "Learn Twi (Akan) 🇬🇭 · lessons, AI translate, Sankofa Lens."
- **Hashtags:** 5–10 in the first comment, mixing niche + mid-size (skip mega-tags where you'll drown): #Twi #LearnTwi #Akan #Ghanaian #Diaspora #TwiLanguage #Accra #Ghana #YearOfReturn.
- **Location tags** (Accra, London, Bronx, Toronto) on relevant posts.
- **Jump on trending audio early** — it's the cheapest reach.

---

## 3. Engagement & community (manual, high-ROI early)

- 20–30 min/day: leave *thoughtful* comments on big Ghana / afrobeats / diaspora accounts **before** you post, so your profile gets return visits.
- Reply to **every** comment and DM fast — early engagement rate is what the algorithm rewards.
- Turn your 10 testers into a street team: ask each to follow, share one Reel to their story, and tag a friend.

---

## 4. Seeding & collabs (where your first 1,000 come from)

- **Micro-creators** (5k–50k): Ghanaian/diaspora "reconnecting with roots" creators, Afrobeats dance pages, language-learning accounts. Gift them Premium + ask for one honest Lens demo. Micro > mega for conversion.
- **Communities:** r/Ghana on Reddit, diaspora Facebook groups, Ghanaian student associations abroad, Detty December / Ghana-travel accounts, WhatsApp community groups.
- **Cross-post** every Reel to TikTok and YouTube Shorts — same file, 3x the surface area, and TikTok is where Twi/diaspora content pops.

---

## 5. Build a viral loop with your own product

- You already shipped **Invite & Earn** (100 pedis when a friend finishes their first lesson) and a weekly **leaderboard**. Promote them: "Learn with friends," "climb the leaderboard," referral CTA in captions.
- Encourage **leaderboard screenshots** and streak flexes — user-generated proof is free reach.

---

## 6. Ride the moments

- **World Cup (Ghana vs Colombia)** and **Detty December** — you have the campaign kits. Cluster content around match dates and the Nov–Dec travel wave; boost only the winners.

---

## Weekly routine (~2–3 hrs total)

- **Mon:** batch-film 3–4 Reels.
- **Tue–Sat:** 1 Reel/day + one Story set; 20 min community engagement; reply to everything.
- **Fri:** post a carousel (saves).
- **Sun:** review metrics, plan next week's hooks.

## The only metric that matters right now

Not followers. Track **bio-link taps → installs → first lesson completed.** A viral Reel that adds zero testers is a vanity win. Optimize the CTA and the one-tap install link until that path converts.
