# Sankofa Twi — 10 Ready-to-Film Reel Scripts (2 weeks)

Phone-shot, 10–20s each, 9:16. Rules for all: **hook in the first 1s**, burned-in captions, trending afrobeats/amapiano audio low under voice, end on the CTA card (`reel/5_endcard.png`). Cross-post each to TikTok + YouTube Shorts. CTA everywhere: **"Learn Twi free — link in bio."**

---

### 1. Sankofa Lens — "Name your lunch" (demo)
- **Hook (0–1s):** "POV: you can now name anything in Twi 🇬🇭"
- **Shots:** Raise phone at a plate of food → app scans → overlay `reel/2_label_food.png` (Aduane · Food) with audio → you repeat it.
- **VO:** "Aduane. Food. I didn't even open a textbook."
- **Caption:** Anything you can see, you can say. #LearnTwi #SankofaLens #Twi #Ghana

### 2. Word of the Day — Akwaaba (talking head)
- **Hook:** "The first Twi word you need before you land in Ghana."
- **Shots:** You to camera, warm. Say "Akwaaba" → text pops "ah-KWAA-ba · Welcome."
- **VO:** "Akwaaba means welcome. Say it back with a smile and watch faces light up."
- **Caption:** One word a day. #Twi #Akan #WordOfTheDay #Ghana

### 3. "Things you'll hear at a Ghanaian house" (relatable list)
- **Hook:** "3 things you'll hear the second you walk into a Ghanaian house 😅"
- **Shots:** Quick cuts, you acting each out. Text overlays: "Woaba? (You've come?)" · "Didi! (Eat!)" · "Wo ho te sɛn? (How are you?)"
- **Caption:** Tag someone whose auntie does #3. #Ghanaian #Twi #Diaspora #Akan

### 4. "Diaspora tries to speak Twi" (funny/relatable)
- **Hook:** "Rating my own Twi as a diaspora kid 💀"
- **Shots:** You attempt a phrase, wince, then the app plays the correct audio, you nail it.
- **VO:** "We're learning together. No shame in the game."
- **Caption:** It's never too late to go back and fetch it. #Sankofa #Twi #Diaspora

### 5. Mispronunciation fix — Ɛ / Ɔ sounds
- **Hook:** "You're saying these Twi words wrong (I was too)."
- **Shots:** Show word "Ɛte sɛn?" → common wrong way vs app's correct audio, side by side.
- **Caption:** The Ɛ sound trips everyone up. #LearnTwi #Twi #Akan #Pronunciation

### 6. Your Akan day name (interactive)
- **Hook:** "Your Ghanaian name is hidden in your birthday. Here's how 👇"
- **Shots:** You explain kra din; overlay a mini table (Mon–Sat → names). Ask "What day were you born?"
- **CTA:** "Find yours in the app."
- **Caption:** Drop your day below and I'll tell you your Akan name. #Akan #Ghana #DayNames

### 7. Adinkra meaning — Sankofa (story)
- **Hook:** "The meaning behind our name will hit different."
- **Shots:** Show the Sankofa glyph → "Se wo were fi na wosan kofa a yenkyiri" → translation.
- **VO:** "Sankofa. Go back and fetch it. It's never too late to reclaim what's yours — starting with the language."
- **Caption:** #Sankofa #Adinkra #Ghana #Twi #Heritage

### 8. Greetings by time of day (quick teach)
- **Hook:** "Greet like a local from morning to night."
- **Shots:** Three quick scenes (morning coffee / midday / evening): "Maakye · Maaha · Maadwo."
- **Caption:** Save this for your trip. #Twi #Akan #Ghana #Greetings

### 9. "Learn with friends" (referral loop)
- **Hook:** "Learning a language alone is hard. So don't."
- **Shots:** You + a friend doing a lesson, laughing, comparing streaks/leaderboard.
- **VO:** "Invite a friend, you both earn. First to finish a lesson wins bragging rights."
- **Caption:** Tag your learning partner 👇 #LearnTwi #Twi #Ghana #LanguageLearning

### 10. World Cup tie-in — "Talk noise in Twi"
- **Hook:** "Ghana vs Colombia — can you talk noise in Twi? 🇬🇭⚽"
- **Shots:** Jersey on, point at screen, deliver "Yɛbɛdi nkonim!" → text "We will win." Use `campaign/worldcup_cover.png` as the opener and `worldcup_endcard.png` to close.
- **Caption:** Don't just watch Ghana. Talk like Ghana. #Ghana #BlackStars #WorldCup #Twi
