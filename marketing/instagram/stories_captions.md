# Sankofa Twi — Story Slide Captions & Sticker Cues

Stories don't have a caption box like feed posts — you add a **text sticker** on the slide and, ideally, an **interactive sticker** (poll / quiz / question / link). Interactive stickers boost reach and give you replies to DM. Below: the short text to add on each slide + the sticker to use.

> On any slide you can add the **Link sticker → sankofaapp.io** to drive installs. Always do it on Start, Lens, and Beta.

## Start
- **start/1** — Text: "Akwaaba 🇬🇭 Welcome to Sankofa Twi — learn Twi the joyful way. Tap through 👉"
  Sticker: **Link → sankofaapp.io.**

---

### Quick how-to for stickers
While creating the Story: tap the **sticker icon** (square smiley, top) → choose **Link**, **Poll**, **Quiz**, or **Questions** → for Link, paste `sankofaapp.io` and (optionally) rename the button text to "Get the app". Position it over the button already drawn on the slide.
