# Sankofa Twi — IG Setup Guide (World Cup post + Highlights)

## Part 0 — Get the images onto your phone
Instagram posting/highlights are easiest from the phone app.
1. On your Mac, the files are in `SankofaTwi.0.v.0.2/marketing/instagram/`.
2. Send them to your phone: select the images → **AirDrop** to your iPhone (or drag into Google Photos / a shared album on Android).
3. They're numbered (`1_cover`, `2_phrase`…) so they stay in order in your camera roll.

---

## Part 1 — Post the World Cup carousel (organic)

**Files:** `carousel/1_cover.png … 5_cta.png` (5 images).

1. AirDrop all 5 carousel images to your phone.
2. Open **Instagram → + (top right) → Post**.
3. Tap the **layers icon** (multiple-select), then tap the images **in order: 1 → 2 → 3 → 4 → 5**. The number badge shows your order — double-check it reads 1,2,3,4,5.
4. Tap **Next**. Skip filters (they're already branded) → **Next**.
5. **Caption** — paste:
   > Don't just watch Ghana. Talk like Ghana. 🇬🇭⚽ Swipe for 3 match-day phrases in Twi to talk noise before Ghana vs Colombia. Learn to say them (properly) free before kickoff 👇 link in bio.
   > #Ghana #BlackStars #WorldCup #Twi #Akan #LearnTwi #Ghanaian #DiasporaTwi #GhanaFootball
6. Put 3–5 extra hashtags in the **first comment** instead of stuffing the caption.
7. **Share.** Post it 2–4 days before the match so it has time to circulate.

**Reel (day-of the match):** you need ~10–15s of footage for a Reel.
1. Film yourself/friends in jerseys reacting on match day.
2. In **CapCut**: put `campaign/worldcup_cover.png` as the first 1–2s, your footage in the middle, `campaign/worldcup_endcard.png` as the last 2–3s. Add trending afrobeats audio + burned-in captions. Export 1080×1920.
3. Instagram **→ + → Reel → upload the video → pick a cover → paste a short caption → Share.**

> Tip: put `sankofaapp.io` as your **bio link** first (Edit Profile → Links), so "link in bio" works.

## Part 2 — Set up Highlights (6 covers)

**Files:** `highlights/1_start.png … 6_beta.png`.
Highlights pull from your **Stories**, so each highlight needs at least one Story slide of content, plus you upload the matching cover.

### A. First, put content in each highlight (post as Stories)
Ready-made 9:16 Story slides are in `stories/<highlight>/`. Just upload them:
- **Start** → `stories/start/1.png`
- **Words** → `stories/words/1–3.png` (Akwaaba, Medaase, Ɛte sɛn?)
- **Adinkra** → `stories/adinkra/1–3.png` (Sankofa, Gye Nyame, Dwennimmen)
- **Culture** → `stories/culture/1–2.png` (day names, greetings)
- **Lens** → `stories/lens/1.png`
- **Beta** → `stories/beta/1.png` — **add a Link sticker** (sankofaapp.io) over the button before sharing.

To post a Story: **+ → Story → select the slide → (on Beta, tap the sticker icon → Link → paste sankofaapp.io) → Share to Story.**

### B. Create each highlight and set its cover
Do this **in reverse order** (Beta first → Start last), because the newest highlight shows on the **left**. Creating Start last puts it in the first slot.

For each highlight:
1. Go to your **Profile → tap "New" under Story Highlights** (the + circle).
2. Select the Story slide(s) for that pillar → **Next**.
3. **Name it** exactly: Beta / Lens / Culture / Adinkra / Words / Start.
4. Tap **Edit Cover → choose the picture icon (gallery/upload) →** pick the matching cover (`6_beta.png`, etc.) → it's pre-centered, just confirm → **Done → Add**.
5. Repeat for all six, in this order: **Beta → Lens → Culture → Adinkra → Words → Start.**

Result: a clean row reading **Start · Words · Adinkra · Culture · Lens · Beta**, each with a gold Adinkra/icon cover.

> Editing later: tap and hold a highlight → **Edit Highlight** to change the cover, add/remove slides, or rename.
